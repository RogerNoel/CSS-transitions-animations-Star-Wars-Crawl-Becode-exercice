A New Hope

It is a period of civil war on the job market. Rebel entrepeneurs, striking from a hidden base, have won their first victory against the evil Traditional Education Administration (TEAd).

During the battle, the Rebel managed to gather a small team of apprentices at their new HQ in Liège, Belgium.

Pursued by the Empire’s sinister agents, Princess Leia races home aboard her starship, custodian of the stolen plans that can save her people and restore freedom to the galaxy…
